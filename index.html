✨ Delhi One-Day Itinerary (Starting & Ending at Bahadurgarh Metro) ✨

8:00 AM – Start

Leave Bahadurgarh Metro Station → Green Line → Kirti Nagar → Blue Line → Rajiv Chowk → Yellow Line → Chandni Chowk.

9:30 AM – Red Fort

Explore Diwan-i-Aam, Diwan-i-Khas, Rang Mahal, museums.
✔ Open: 9:30 AM – 4:30 PM (Closed Monday)

11:15 AM – Bhogal Chole Bhature

Travel to CP → Have famous Delhi-style chole bhature.

12:00 PM – Gurudwara Bangla Sahib

Peaceful noon stop, sarovar + spiritual vibes.

12:50 PM – Agrasen ki Baoli

Historic stepwell + photo spot.

1:30 PM – India Gate

Walk around, take photos, enjoy Rajpath area.

2:15 PM – National Museum

Explore ancient India collections.
✔ Open: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Closed Monday)

3:50 PM – Connaught Place Snacks

Dolma Aunty Momos (Half Fried), Depaul’s Cold Coffee, quick street food break.

4:30 PM – National Science Centre

Interactive exhibits, space, tech, and science fun.
✔ Open: 9:30 AM – 6:00 PM

6:30 PM – Chandni Chowk Snacks

Kuremal Kulfi, Natraj Dahi Bhalla, local momos, parathas.

7:00 PM – Qutub Minar

Sunset photography, evening atmosphere.
✔ Open till sunset (core hours end 5 PM)

8:15 PM – Dinner near Qutub Metro

WOW Momos, Biryani Blues, street snacks, cafes.

9:00 PM – Return

Metro back → Qutub Minar → Kirti Nagar → Bahadurgarh.

10:20 PM – End

Arrive back at Bahadurgarh Metro Station.
